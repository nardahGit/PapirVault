Updated: Mar 2026

A record must come from an authorized issuing officer, include a traceable reference ID, clear issue date, document type, and supporting legal basis. Records without legal context should remain in draft status.

Issue a new version with a new reference ID, mark the prior version as superseded, and retain both entries in the audit trail. Avoid overwriting historical records.

Reject when core fields are missing, reference IDs are invalid, consent is absent, or legal basis is unclear. These conditions prevent lawful verification and should trigger re-submission.

Keep upload metadata, verification outcomes, decision timestamps, and operator identity. Retention must follow your institutional policy and applicable legal timelines.
1. Purpose and Scope

This policy governs how issuer organizations process personal and document-related data in PapirVault for lawful issuance and verification.

2. Lawful Processing

Issuers must only upload records with a valid legal basis, purpose limitation, and authorized operational need. Data entry without legal context is prohibited.

3. Data Minimization

Upload only fields required for verification: reference ID, client identifier, document type, issue/expiry dates, and legal note. Avoid excessive or unrelated personal data.

4. Access and Confidentiality

Access must be role-based. Staff accounts should use strong authentication, and issuer teams must regularly review inactive access.

5. Retention and Deletion

Documents and logs are retained according to issuer legal obligations and internal records policy. Expired or superseded records require controlled archival.

6. Incident Reporting

Any suspected misuse, unauthorized access, or integrity issue must be escalated immediately to compliance/security officers and logged for audit review.

Verification Standard

A document is considered well verified only when uploaded metadata matches verification input, dates are valid, legal basis is present, and issuer confirmation is recorded.

Operational Checklist
  • Confirm the source upload reference exists.
  • Match client ID and document type exactly.
  • Confirm issue date is not after expiry date.
  • Ensure legal note references authority or policy.
  • Capture final decision and operator identity.
